Tend

Support

Tend is a pet care journal: log the real things you do for your pets, keep routines on streak, and watch their hand-drawn avatar level up. Need a hand? Answers to common questions are below.

Fastest way to reach us: tendpettracker@gmail.com — we read everything.

Getting started

Create an account (or sign in with Apple), add your first pet, and start logging — every walk, meal, or grooming session earns your pet XP and earns you coins to spend in the Treat Shop.

Routines & streaks

  • Create a routine on the Routines tab — daily, on specific weekdays (like Mon · Wed · Fri), or once a week, at a set time or anytime.
  • Edit a routine by tapping it; reorder or delete with a long-press.
  • Streaks continue as long as you complete each scheduled day — skipped days that aren't scheduled don't break them.

Reminders

Turn on reminders in Profile → Routine reminders, and pick a timing (up to 30 minutes before or after each routine's set time). Notifications are scheduled locally on your device — if they aren't appearing, check Settings → Notifications → Tend on your iPhone.

Fixing mistakes

  • Accidental log? Long-press the entry in the Journal to remove it — XP, coins, and streaks are rolled back automatically.
  • Rename a pet or yourself from the Profile tab.

Your data & account

Your data syncs to your account, so signing in on a new phone restores everything. You can permanently delete your account and all data anytime in Profile → Delete account. Details are in the privacy policy.

Found a bug? Want a feature?

Email tendpettracker@gmail.com with what happened (screenshots help!) and we'll get on it.
